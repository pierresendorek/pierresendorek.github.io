Pierre SENDOREK is a Data Science consultant working at Xebia, where he is also recruiter and formator in the domain of neural networks. In 2015, he obtained a PhD in Applied Mathematics and Signal Processing. He also holds an engineering diploma, which he obtained in 2009 from Télécom ParisTech, the leading school in signal processing in France, as well as a master 1 degree in mathematics, which he obtained in 2006 from the University of Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée (UPEM).
